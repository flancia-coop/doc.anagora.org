**Dupliter** — a conceptual celestial twin to Jupiter, proposed by Ashlan Chidester.  
Reference: *Dupliter: A Conceptual Approach to Temporal Anchoring* (MC Inc, 1999). DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.15190883.  
Identifier: DOI [[10.5281/zenodo.15190883]] • ORCID [[0009-0005-5880-1614]]

**Abstract:** Dupliter models a hypothetical mirror Jupiter to investigate temporal symmetry, nonlinear gravitation, cosmic speeds, and metaphysical time reflection.

> *“What Jupiter conceals in orbit, Dupliter reveals in recurrence.”*

Dupliter describes a theoretical Jupiter construct of temporal anchoring and gravitational reflection used to explore nonlinear time in astrophysics.
